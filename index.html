Our Story

For over 100 years, my family has been making delicious, nourishing chicken broth. Born in the Lower East Side, our broth comes from a simpler time, when nothing - not even leftover chicken bones or veggie scraps - was wasted. With patience and care, these humble ingredients transform into something truly greater than the sum of its parts.

Healthy food is real food, and this broth has been enjoyed for generations by real people seeking warmth, comfort, and a taste of home.

We take the time to simmer our broth slowly, then freeze it fresh - the best way to lock in flavor - so you can put nourishing food on the table quickly, without shortcuts.

From our family to yours, we know you're going to love it. You can trust us, the broth is in our bones.

Health & Nourishment

Lucky Bone Broth is real food that happens to be ridiculously healthy. Nothing hidden, never any preservatives. What makes ours different is simple: we freeze it fresh, locking in the flavors and nutrients that take hours to cultivate.

When you eat Lucky Bone, you're getting the real thing — no gimmicks, no trends. Just whole foods, always.

Our broth is naturally rich in collagen, gelatin, and amino acids - compounds that support gut health, joint mobility, and healthy skin. Sip or Soup, this broth never disappoints.

Recipes & Pairings

Morning Sipper — Heat one cup of Classic Chicken Broth, add a squeeze of lemon. Sip slowly. Start well.

Sunday Soup — Sauté garlic and leeks in butter, add two cups of our broth, a handful of orzo, and fresh dill. Ready in 20 minutes.

Classic Chicken Noodle — Bring two cups of Classic Chicken to a simmer, add egg noodles, shredded chicken, and a handful of fresh dill. Ready in 15 minutes, tastes like it took all day.
